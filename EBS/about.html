About Us

EBS is an Intellectual Property firm, specializing in the protection and internationalization of trademarks, patents, Brand Protection, copyright, and domains.

Experience

With over 5 years of experience in Intellectual Property, EBS has served thousands of clients ranging from small entrepreneurs and startups to multinational corporations with large trademark and patent portfolios and other entities involved in R&D on a daily basis. Furthermore, because we maintain relationships with clients from various sectors, Brand Protection, including food and beverage companies, communications, IT, pharmaceutical, manufacturers, oil and gas companies, financial institutions, business services companies, and more, our experience allows us to understand the caveats of the different industries.

Technical Drive

EBS recognizes that information management and the internet have taken over many aspects of business today. To meet our client’s changing needs, we have increased our activity in the areas of technological infrastructures and information systems in recent years. EBS seeks to respond to the various demands of the market and its clients in this manner.

Vision

Our team has the skills, experience and expertise to fight against IPR infringement by developing creative solutions to protect brands from counterfeits. We are a team of IPR experts with extensive knowledge in the field of Intellectual Property Rights (IPR), including patents, trademarks, copyrights and trade secrets. We have established ourselves as a reputable brand protection agency that delivers exceptional outcomes for our clients.

Our role is to deliver excellence in combating counterfeits/illicit/look-alike/illegal products to defense the damage and prejudice of our clients. Also help brands and stand at the forefront to contain any counterfeit against these and move with velocity to drive substantial growth while maintaining brand image and standards.
